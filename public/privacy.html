Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Crickle ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle data when you use the Crickle mobile application and website.

1. Data Collection and Storage

Crickle is designed to be a private, serverless experience. We do not require you to create an account, and we do not collect, transmit, or store your personal data on any external servers.

All of your gameplay data, including your stats, win streaks, and your chosen Head-to-Head username, is stored locally on your device (using LocalStorage).

2. Third-Party Services (Ads)

To keep the game free, we use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may use advertising IDs and cookies to serve personalized ads based on your interests.

  • You can learn more about how Google uses information from sites or apps that use their services here.

3. Head-to-Head Challenges

When you share a challenge link with a friend, your chosen username and game score are encoded directly into the URL you share. This information is passed directly to whoever clicks your link; it is never routed through or saved on our servers.

4. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
