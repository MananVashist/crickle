Frequently Asked Questions

My stats disappeared!

Your stats are stored locally on your device. If you clear your browser cache, reinstall the app, or switch devices, your stats will reset. We do not use servers to sync data in order to protect your privacy.

Why isn't my favorite player in the game?

To keep the game balanced, we currently only feature players from Test-playing nations. Additionally, Test players must have debuted after 1980, and ODI players after 1990 (unless they are widely recognized icons of the sport).

How does the Daily Puzzle work?

The Daily Puzzle features a challenging, curated player that is the exact same for everyone in the world. It rotates formats and resets at midnight IST.
